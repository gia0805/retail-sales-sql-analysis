## Summary Insights

- The dataset contains 1,000 total transactions from 1,000 unique customers, generating total revenue of 456,000.
- Electronics generates the highest total revenue (156,905), slightly ahead of Clothing (155,580).
- Clothing has the most transactions (351) but the lowest average order value (443.25), indicating high-volume, lower-price purchases.
- Beauty has the highest average order value (467.48) with fewer transactions (307), indicating fewer but higher-value purchases.
- Monthly revenue varies across 2023, with May 2023 as the strongest month (105 transactions, 53,150 revenue).
- January 2024 shows minimal activity and appears to be a partial month, so it was excluded from trend interpretation.
